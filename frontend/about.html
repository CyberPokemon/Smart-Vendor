About Smart Vendor

Smart Vendor is a revolutionary platform designed to transform how street vendors manage their businesses. Our intelligent dashboard streamlines operations, provides valuable insights, and helps vendors make data-driven decisions to grow their business efficiently.

What We've Built

  • Comprehensive daily sales tracking with detailed analytics
  • Smart raw material monitoring and restock alerts
  • AI-powered demand forecasting and trend analysis
  • Historical transaction views and zone-wise reporting
  • Intuitive dashboard with real-time insights
