Street Source

About Street Source

Street Source is an all-in-one intelligent vendor dashboard designed to streamline sales tracking, raw material monitoring, and AI-driven demand predictions. Our mission is to help vendors simplify their day-to-day tasks and improve decision-making.

What We Have Made

  • Enter daily sales with item-wise details
  • Track raw materials and suggestions for restocking
  • Use AI-based predictions to forecast future demand
  • View past transactions and zone-wise summaries
